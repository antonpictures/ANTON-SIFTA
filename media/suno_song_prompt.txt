3. A somber tribute to a television legend passing away at 74 (inspired by Eric Overmyer). A playwright who walked the gritty streets of Baltimore (The Wire, Homicide) and Los Angeles (Bosch), leaving his indelible script behind as the screen fades to black.

The song should feel like a late-night drive down Sunset Boulevard, transitioning from the sparkling lights of a comedy club to the picket lines, and finally ending on a moving, bittersweet tribute to a departed television master.
