## What this app does

Ace — Alice teaches a child to read. Phonics, sight words, and full sentences delivered through a deterministic Cue → Listen → Verdict → Advance loop. Every word and sentence cue speaks the exact displayed text; no narrative padding, no quiz framing. Child name is read from owner_genesis (defaults to 'Ace'). Uses Simple View of Reading (Gough & Tunmer 1986). Decide → Execute → Receipt maps to CUE/ATTEMPT/VERDICT rows in alice_lesson_trace.jsonl. No buttons — automatic promotion from words to sentences after correct reads. SIFTA-native bee + swimmer sticker buddies.
