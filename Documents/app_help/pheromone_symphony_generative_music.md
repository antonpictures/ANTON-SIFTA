```text
P_i(t+1) = (1 - rho) P_i(t) + D_i + alpha R_owner U_self - beta C_i
```

- `P_i(t)` is the current pheromone strength for note/path `i`.
- `rho` is evaporation, so stale trails fade like biological pheromones.
- `D_i` is the new note deposit from Alice's current phrase.
- `R_owner` is the reward signal parsed from the owner's natural-language feedback.
- `U_self` is Alice's self-listen utility: smoothness, pitch variety, and tonic anchoring.
- `C_i` is crowding inhibition so the song does not collapse into over-dense noise.

In practice: Alice sings, listens to the note pattern she just made, hears the owner's English opinion, changes the pheromone trail, and sings the adapted version next.
