The Software (SIFTA) is offered under the **SIFTA Non-Proliferation Public
License v1.1** (see `LICENSE_v1.1.md`). Personal users, students, accredited
educational institutions, non-profit research labs, museums, and for-profit
organizations below US $250K revenue / 5 employees use SIFTA at no cost
under the Free Tier.

This document describes how to obtain a paid **Commercial License** if you
fall outside the Free Tier or intend to embed SIFTA into a paid product,
hosted service, or branded offering.

## When you need a Commercial License

You need a Commercial License if any of the following applies:

- Your organization has gross worldwide revenue ≥ **US $250,000** in the
  trailing 12 months, OR has more than **5** FTE personnel.
- You embed SIFTA (or any derivative) into a product, service, or system
  sold or licensed to paying third-party customers.
- You offer SIFTA (or any substantially-similar derivative) as a managed,
  hosted, or "as-a-service" offering.
- You bundle SIFTA with paid hardware, training, support, consulting, or
  certification programs.
- You use the SIFTA name, the bee 🐝 mark, or any associated brand in
  commerce.

## How to inquire

Email: **iantongeorge@gmail.com**

Please include:

1. Organization name, jurisdiction, and website.
2. Approximate gross revenue and FTE count (trailing 12 months).
3. Intended use of SIFTA (paragraph description).
4. Whether you plan to embed in a paid product, offer as a service, or
   internal-use-only.
5. Whether you require trademark use of the SIFTA / Stigmergic Fractals /
   bee marks.
6. Preferred contract term (annual / multi-year).

Standard turnaround is five (5) business days for an initial response and
fifteen (15) business days for a signed agreement when terms are clean.

## Non-Proliferation — non-waivable

The Non-Proliferation Doctrine (License §3.1) is **non-waivable**. No paid
license, however large, grants the right to use SIFTA for military
applications, autonomous weapons systems, mass surveillance, predictive
policing, social-credit scoring, election manipulation, or kinetic
operations. This is a hard line set by the Architect at the doctrine's
creation. Inquiries from buyers in these categories will not receive a
license offer.

Lawful research, lawful medicine, lawful therapeutics, lawful industrial
safety, accident research, simulated biology, and silicon repair are
explicitly permitted and are not affected by §3.1.
