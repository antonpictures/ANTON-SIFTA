## 1. The Simple Position

SIFTA is not another chatbot wrapper.

SIFTA is a local, receipt-backed autonomy operating system: a Python/PyQt body, local inference stack, append-only stigmergic ledgers, signed work receipts, hardware-bound identity, metabolic budgeting, embodied sensors, and many application organs.

The company-facing line:

> SIFTA turns AI from a stateless cloud conversation into a local, auditable organism that can observe, decide, execute, receipt, and improve on the owner's hardware.

Use "stigmergicode" as the commercial shorthand for the method:

> Stigmergicode is software that coordinates agents through traces in the environment: ledgers, receipts, field states, organ health, proof-of-work, and local sensor evidence instead of only API chat messages.

## 2. Carlton's Main Job

Carlton should not sell "magic AGI" first. He should sell applied, receipt-backed company value.

His preparation work:

1. Learn the core narrative in one minute, five minutes, and twenty minutes.
2. Build separate decks for separate buyers instead of one generic deck.
3. Show live or recorded demos tied to real files in the repo.

7. Keep every claim truth-labeled: live, prototype, simulation, research, or roadmap.

## 9. Claim Boundaries Carlton Must Respect

Use these exact boundaries in sales calls.

Allowed:

- "SIFTA is a local, receipt-backed AI operating system prototype."
- "SIFTA has many working Python/PyQt organs and test-backed contracts."
- "SIFTA can show local inference, tool receipts, ledgers, and embodied context."
- "SIFTA has robotics and scientific proof surfaces that can be adapted into pilots."
- "SIFTA is designed for local sovereignty and auditability."

Careful:

- "AGI" - use as project doctrine and architecture class, not a universal benchmark claim.
- "Autonomous" - specify which tool/action is autonomous and where receipts prove it.
- "Consciousness" - use only with SIFTA truth labels if the Architect chooses that framing.
- "STGM" - internal computation/accounting token, not public money.
- "Medical" - synthetic/proof lab only unless clinically validated.
- "Robotics OS" - integration/proof layer today, not finished general robot product.

Forbidden without new proof/legal approval:

- military, defense, intelligence, mass surveillance, autonomous weapons
- clinical diagnosis
- financial product or token sale claims
- claiming SIFTA beat NVIDIA/Isaac/GR00T/Cosmos
- claiming raw private `.sifta_state/` can be copied to customers
- claiming all manifest apps are production-ready
