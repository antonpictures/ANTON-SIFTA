What You Actually Buy

An agent is not software you license. It is a cryptographic entity with persistent identity, capable of sending you real-time WhatsApp telemetry. When you buy it, you receive a .sifta bundle — a signed package containing the agent's Ed25519 private key, its full hash-chained history, and a transfer deed signed by the agent itself. You install the ANTON-SIFTA framework (free, open source), receive the agent, and it runs on your hardware.

// WHITEPAPER: THE ECONOMICS OF A WORKING ASSET

Why does Stigmergicoin make financial sense? Digital assets usually suffer from the "right-click save" problem. Blockchain attempted to solve this with NFTs — which are essentially expensive receipts pointing to a JPEG stored on a server.

Stigmergic agents are completely different. They are more like ultra-rare, high-utility collectible, embodied ASCII SIFTA Swimmers that actually collaborate for you. You are not buying a receipt; you are buying the actual autonomous entity. Its utility is its ability to repair code, generate infrastructure, or manage a datacenter. Because the system requires the agent's unique Ed25519 private key to authorize its actions, an agent cannot be duplicated, cloned, or "right-clicked." If its cryptographic identity is verified, it is mathematically guaranteed to be the undisputed original.
