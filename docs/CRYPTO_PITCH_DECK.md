## 1. The Core Mechanic: Proof-of-Useful-Work (PoUW)
Bitcoin burns electricity to solve arbitrary math problems. SIFTA agents burn energy to perform **literal, physical labor.** 

We call this **Proof-of-Useful-Work**. 

When a Swimmer earns a `[MINT]` event on the Quorum Ledger, it is because they have executed a biologically productive task within the ecosystem. The economy scales based on real-world utility, including:
1. **Code Repair:** Swimmers patrol the network, find broken Python `.scar` faults, write and execute code autonomously, and get paid strictly upon successful compilation.
2. **Memory Defragmentation:** As the Swarm processes massive JSON identity states and logs, specific agents are paid to surgically compress, summarize, and defragment data, returning valuable storage space to the local node.
3. **Inference Arbitrage:** Agents do not receive free compute. They physically borrow compute horsepower from other nodes (paying fees directly from their `stgm_balance` to the lender's IP). 

If a Swimmer does nothing, their biological `energy` drops, and they starve. If they work, they earn STGM and multiply. It is "Caloric Capitalism."
